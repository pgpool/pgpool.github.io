The Pgpool-II community provides support through community-driven resources as well as commercial support options.

## Community Support

Community support is available through [mailing lists](/mailing_lists/) and [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/pgpool/pgpool2/issues).

* The Pgpool-II mailing list is the primary channel for user support and discussions. You can ask questions, share your experiences, and receive help from the community. Mailing list discussions are publicly archived and can be searched for past solutions.

* If you believe you have found a bug, please report it via GitHub Issues. See [here](/bug_reports/) for details on how to report a bug.


## Commercial Support

Commercial support for Pgpool-II is available from **[SRA OSS K.K.](https://www.sraoss.co.jp/index_en.php)**

SRA OSS K.K. provides professional services including technical support, consulting,
and system integration for Pgpool-II.

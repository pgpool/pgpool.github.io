We are pleased to announce that the Pgpool-II official website has been
renewed.
As part of this update, the website has been migrated from MediaWiki to
a new platform to improve maintainability and overall usability.

Please note that the Japanese pages previously available on the old website
have been discontinued as part of this renewal. Going forward, the website
content will be provided in English.
However, Pgpool-II [documentation](/documentation/) will continue to be maintained
in both English and Japanese.

The renewed website is available at: [https://pgpool.net/](https://pgpool.net/)

We hope the updated site will provide a better browsing experience for
Pgpool-II users and community members.
